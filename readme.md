## Project Overview

Three Wishes Bakery is a California-based, nurse-owned small business specializing in homemade comfort food and local community catering.

This was a full-scope client engagement — not a template build. The work spanned brand strategy, UX/UI design, front-end engineering, and conversion copywriting, delivered as a cohesive launch package for a real business entering a competitive local market.

---

## Strategic Brief

The core positioning challenge: the client needed to stand out from oversized luxury dessert trends without competing on price alone.

The solution was intentional brand differentiation — anchoring the identity in nostalgia, warmth, and authenticity rather than aesthetics or prestige. Every design and copy decision reinforced that positioning.

**Brand pillars defined for this project:**
- Nostalgic homemade comfort
- Affordability and accessibility
- Local community connection
- Nurse-owned authenticity

---

## Key Outcomes

A complete digital presence built to support real business operations — local delivery, pickup coordination, catering inquiries, and brand credibility for a first-time small business owner entering the market.

The project was designed for scalability: structured to support future growth, expanded product lines, and e-commerce integration without a rebuild.

---

## Approach

This project reflects a belief that good technical work and good brand strategy are the same discipline in different clothes. The front-end engineering served the story. The story served the customer. The customer served the business.

Small business clients — especially first-time owners — need a builder who can hold the full picture. That's what this engagement was.
