Nurse Jeanie's cookie care

Made with care. Kept with care.

A little nurse's advice for keeping every batch as fresh as the moment it left the oven.

Cookie care

Warm like it's fresh.

Airtight container up to 5 days at room temp, or freeze up to 3 months. Bring back that just-baked hug: 350°F oven for 5–7 min, or microwave 10–15 sec (+20 sec if frozen).

Take-&-bake dough

Keep cold. Bake hot.

Cookie Dough Sleeves live in the freezer. Preheat to 350°F, arrange pucks on a lined sheet, and bake straight from frozen for warm-from-Jeanie's-kitchen magic.

Shipping & allergens
