Nurse Jeanie's cookie care

Made with care. Kept with care.

A little nurse's advice for keeping every batch as fresh as the moment it left the oven.

Cookie care

Warm like it's fresh.

Airtight container up to 5 days at room temp, or freeze up to 3 months. Bring back that just-baked hug: 350°F oven for 5–7 min, or microwave 10–15 sec (+20 sec if frozen).

Take-&-bake dough

Keep cold. Bake hot.

Cookie Dough Sleeves live in the freezer. Preheat to 350°F, arrange pucks on a lined sheet, and bake straight from frozen for warm-from-Jeanie's-kitchen magic.

Shipping & allergens

Shipped with care.

Orders ship Mondays & Wednesdays in temperature-controlled packaging, 1–2 business day delivery. Contains wheat, milk, eggs, and soy. May contain traces of nuts.

Allergy information — please read

Three Wishes Bakery is not a nut-free, peanut-free, gluten-free, dairy-free, or allergen-free kitchen. Our treats contain or may come into contact with wheat, milk, eggs, soy, peanuts, and tree nuts. Flavors rotate weekly, so please review each product's details before ordering. If you have a severe allergy, our kitchen may not be the safest option for you — and we'd rather tell you that honestly.
